Why Unicode should be standard on all websites

Unicode should be standard on all websites because people from anywhere in the world can access the website regardless of which languages they can read. For example English letters that an Australian writes on a forum could be read as Hiragana by someone reading the forum in Japan. This makes the web accessable to everyone no matter where they are.
